They saw Willow as nothing more than an obstacle to progress, blind to the wisdom she held. Willow tried to communicate with them, to make them see the irreplaceable loss they were about to cause. But her words were lost in the noise of their machinery. With each tree that fell, Willow's heart ached. The animals scattered, their homes destroyed, and the once-thriving rainforest turned into a barren wasteland. Willow's spirit faded away, leaving behind a haunting reminder of the consequences of human greed.)
